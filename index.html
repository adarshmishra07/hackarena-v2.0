FAQ's

What is hackathon?

Don’t tell me you don’t know. Hackathon's are super cool events full of development, technology, fun, and start up atmosphere. Attendees(hereinafter referred to as Hackers) work in teams to create awesome solutions: apps, chat-bots, robots or whatever you like!

When?

Hackarena is scheduled on 21st march 2020.

Where?

HackArena will be held at Lokmanya Tilak College Of Engineering, Navi Mumbai.

What skills do I need to participate?

Any skills! We are not just looking for coders! Whether you are a software-engineer, student, designer, or an Iron Man.

What is code of conduct?

Code of Conduct of HackArena 1.0

What about team?

You can have min of 2 and max of 4 members in a team and if you don't have a team we can help you find one !

Can I work on my previous projects?

We apologise, but all hackathon projects should be developed at the event from scratch. We want honest conditions for all hackers. That's why all code should be done during the hackathon.

Will there be food arrangements?

Yes absolutely. You will be provided with meals and refreshments.
